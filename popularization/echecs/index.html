A game

In collaboration with Christoph Müßig and Érika Roldán-Roa, we developed a game on queen and rook domination on polyominoes. Het is accessible on this website https://www.erikaroldan.net/queensrooksdomination. It is companion to some research we did, please do let us know if you find a winning strategy in polynomial time, as those two problems are NP-complete, this can be the start of a fruitful collaboration!

    Research on chess and math

    Our new preprint with Chris Müßig and Érika Róldan-Roa Complexity of chess domination problems is out at arXiv:2211.05651 ! It proves NP-completeness of a few problems of domination by queens and rooks on polycubes. It also boasts a companion video game and the solver we present was used to extend A075324.
