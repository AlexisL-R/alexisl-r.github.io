Welcome to the website of the Kleine seminar! This small seminar takes place at Universiteit Gent and was initiated in summer 2019 to create a space for graduate students and postdoc researchers with shared research interests to study recent subject in depth. The method is quite simple: each semester, the members meet and decide of one subject they want to investigate, then they choose a base reference and divide the subject amongst themselves so that each of them will present a part. It aims to keep an informal ambiance and every kind of questions is welcomed, especially dumb ones!

Subject

We will follow Humphrey's Representations of Semisimple Lie algebras in the BGG category $\mathcal{O}$, zbMATH's review (see for example this group in Columbia that had the same idea https://www.math.columbia.edu/~hliu/seminars/s20-category-o.html ).

We will try to keep the session streamlined: doing only the proofs that add something, maybe do some proof for $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ or $\mathfrak{sl}_3$ if it is not trivial. The audience will try to skim through the material beforehand

Next meeting: Thursday 21th January, 15:00 CET a short review of Semisimple Lie algebras by Roy Oste

We cover the zeroth chapter of the book reviewing notations and material from semisimple Lie algebras.
