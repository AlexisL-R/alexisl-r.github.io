Alexis Langlois-Rémillard

Ph.D. student at Universiteit Gent

Since April 2019, I am working at Universiteit Gent in the broad topic of representation theory of algebras with a focus on operator algebras and application to mathematical physics. I did my bachelor and master degrees at Université de Montréal, the latter under the direction of Yvan Saint-Aubin on the representation theory of seam algebras, a generalization of Temperley-Lieb algebras.

Research interests

I am now working on the algebraic underlying structure of operator algebras. The mathematics areas involved are plenty but I concentrate on representation theory and Lie superalgebras. A long-term goal is to investigate the potential of diagrammatic algebras in this topic via the Hecke algebras framework and quantum algebras. More detailed introductory notes in my research notes.
